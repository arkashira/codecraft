## Business Model Canvas – CodeCraft

### Customer Segments  
- **Non‑technical founders** (startup founders, solo entrepreneurs, small business owners) who need to launch MVPs without hiring developers.  
- **Indie hackers & solo developers** who want to prototype quickly and focus on product‑market fit.  
- **Small‑to‑mid sized agencies** that need rapid prototyping for client pitches.  
- **Educational institutions** (bootcamps, coding schools) looking to teach low‑code development.  

### Value Propositions  
- **Zero‑code, AI‑powered development**: Generate production‑ready code from natural‑language prompts.  
- **Speed & cost savings**: Build MVPs 5× faster and 70 % cheaper than traditional hiring.  
- **Iterative prototyping**: Rapidly test ideas with live deployments and instant feedback loops.  
- **Low learning curve**: Intuitive UI, no prior coding knowledge required.  
- **Scalable architecture**: Seamless migration to full‑stack codebases when the product matures.  

### Channels  
- **Direct web portal** (self‑serve sign‑up, free tier, live demos).  
- **Partnerships with startup accelerators & incubators** (co‑branded workshops).  
- **Content marketing** (tutorial videos, case studies, webinars).  
- **Paid ads on LinkedIn & Reddit (r/entrepreneur, r/indiehackers)** targeting founder personas.  
- **Referral program** for existing users to invite peers.  

### Customer Relationships  
- **Self‑service onboarding** with guided tutorials and AI chat support.  
- **Community forums & Discord** for peer support and feature requests.  
- **Premium support tiers** (email, chat, dedicated success manager).  
- **Regular product updates & roadmap transparency** via newsletters.  
- **Feedback loops**: in‑app surveys and usage analytics to iterate on UX.  

### Revenue Streams  
- **Freemium tier**: Unlimited low‑priority projects, limited AI usage.  
- **Pro tier**: $29 / month (≈ 950 THB) – unlimited projects, priority AI, advanced integrations.  
- **Enterprise tier**: $199 / month (≈ 6 500 THB) – custom SLAs, on‑prem deployment, dedicated support.  
- **Marketplace add‑ons**: $5–$20 per plugin (e.g., payment gateways, analytics).  
- **White‑label licensing**: $1 M / year (≈ 33 M THB) for large enterprises.  

### Key Resources  
- **AI model & training data** (open‑source LLMs fine‑tuned on code corpora).  
- **Cloud infrastructure** (AWS/GCP for compute, storage, CI/CD).  
- **Product & UX teams** (design, engineering, data science).  
- **Customer success & community managers**.  
- **Legal & compliance** (data‑privacy, open‑source licensing).  

### Key Activities  
- **Model training & fine‑tuning** to improve code quality & domain coverage.  
- **Platform development** (UI, API, CI/CD pipelines).  
- **Continuous integration & automated testing** for generated code.  
- **Marketing & sales** (content, webinars, partner outreach).  
- **Customer support & community moderation**.  

### Key Partners  
- **Open‑source LLM providers** (e.g., Hugging Face, OpenAI).  
- **Cloud service providers** (AWS, GCP, Azure) for scalable compute.  
- **Startup accelerators & incubators** (Y Combinator, Techstars).  
- **Payment processors** (Stripe, PayPal) for subscription billing.  
- **Legal & compliance advisors** for open‑source licensing.  

### Cost Structure  
- **Cloud compute & storage**: ~$15 k / month (≈ 500 k THB).  
- **Model licensing & fine‑tuning**: ~$5 k / month (≈ 165 k THB).  
- **Engineering & product salaries**: ~$120 k / month (≈ 4 M THB).  
- **Marketing & sales**: ~$20 k / month (≈ 660 k THB).  
- **Customer support & community**: ~$10 k / month (≈ 330 k THB).  
- **Legal & compliance**: ~$5 k / month (≈ 165 k THB).
