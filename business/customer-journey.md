```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Codecraft

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** Non-technical founder or indie hacker realizes the need to build a software product but lacks coding skills.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about no-code platforms.
- Skepticism about the capabilities of no-code tools.
**User Emotions:**
- Frustration with the complexity of coding.
- Anxiety about the time and effort required to learn coding.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Educational content showcasing success stories of non-technical founders.
- Interactive demos highlighting the ease of use.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to the product website.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** Potential user researches no-code platforms after becoming aware of the option.
**Friction Points:**
- Overwhelming number of options and comparisons.
- Concerns about the limitations of no-code tools.
**User Emotions:**
- Overwhelmed by choices.
- Hopeful but cautious about the effectiveness of the tool.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed comparison charts with competitors.
- Testimonials from satisfied users.
**Metric:** Time spent on the product website and engagement with comparison content.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Potential user signs up for a free trial or demo of Codecraft.
**Friction Points:**
- Complex onboarding process.
- Limited functionality in the free trial.
**User Emotions:**
- Excitement about trying the tool.
- Frustration if the onboarding process is not smooth.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Guided onboarding tutorials.
- Access to a community forum for support.
**Metric:** Conversion rate from trial sign-ups to active users.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** User decides to purchase a subscription after a successful trial.
**Friction Points:**
- Pricing concerns and perceived value.
- Integration issues with existing tools.
**User Emotions:**
- Confidence in the tool's capabilities.
- Anxiety about the long-term commitment.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Flexible pricing plans and discounts for long-term commitments.
- Dedicated customer support for integration issues.
**Metric:** Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV).

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** User achieves initial success and looks to scale their usage.
**Friction Points:**
- Limited advanced features.
- Need for additional training and support.
**User Emotions:**
- Satisfaction with initial results.
- Desire for more advanced capabilities.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Access to advanced features and customization options.
- Ongoing training and support programs.
**Metric:** Revenue from upsells and cross-sells.
```
