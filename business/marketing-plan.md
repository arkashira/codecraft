# 📈 Marketing Plan – **codecraft**  
*No‑code AI‑powered software development platform for non‑technical founders & indie hackers.*

---

## 1️⃣ Positioning  

| # | One‑line Positioning |
|---|----------------------|
| **Primary** | **codecraft** lets founders launch a SaaS product in days instead of months by turning natural‑language prompts into production‑ready code—no coding required. |
| **Alt 1** | Build, test, and ship a full‑stack web app with a single chat, while the AI handles architecture, security, and deployment. |
| **Alt 2** | From idea to live product in 48 h: codecraft’s AI writes, debugs, and hosts your code so you can focus on growth. |
| **Alt 3** | Empower indie hackers to create custom software without hiring engineers—AI writes clean, production‑grade code on demand. |

## 3️⃣ Channels & CAC Estimates  

| Channel | Tactics (first 90 days) | Expected CAC | Rationale |
|---------|------------------------|--------------|-----------|
| **Paid Community Ads** (e.g., Indie Hackers, Product Hunt, SaaS subreddit) | Sponsored posts, carousel ads, “Launch your MVP in 48 h” creatives. | **$45 / user** | High intent audience; CPM ≈ $12, conversion ≈ 0.4 % → $30 + $15 onboarding cost. |
| **Content‑Driven SEO + Guest Posts** | Publish “AI‑no‑code vs. traditional dev” series; guest on popular dev‑tools blogs. | **$22 / user** | Low ongoing spend; organic acquisition after 30 days; conversion from blog → free trial ≈ 1 %. |
| **Referral / Affiliate Program** | 20 % revenue share for each paid conversion; early‑beta ambassadors (YouTubers, podcasts). | **$15 / user** | Incentivizes existing users; high LTV (≈ $1,200) makes low CAC viable. |

*Target Mix*: 45 % Paid Community, 35 % SEO, 20 % Referral → **Weighted CAC ≈ $31**.
